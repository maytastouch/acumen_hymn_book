150. BA KAI BA KUTULI?

(O WHERE ARE THE REAPERS – A.H.360, C.H.620, C.S.548)

 

  1. Ba kai bakutuli ba ba tisa man’ata a mande a zwa ‘simu,

    2. Kutulen’ ka litipa za niti, mu sebeze mane Jesu A te.

     

    MAKUTELO;

    Ba ba kutula bona ba kai?

    Ba to sebeleza Jesu mwa simu;

    Ki man’ ya tusa, ku kutula mabele ‘simu ya Mulena.

     

  2. Zwelen’ lipatelo mu ba zume, mabele a ten’ hala lihula,

    3. Kenen’ linzila mi mu ba tise, mu ba putele hae kwa halimu.

     

  3. Masimu a buzwize kai ni kai, lifasi li yemezi kutulo,

    4. Bakutuli ki ba ba nyinyani, mi musebezi ki o mutuna.

     

  4. Mina batu mute ni litipa, mu kubukanye mabele ‘moho;

    5. Sebezen’ mane Jesu a tahe, mu to abelwa kwa tabo ya Hae.
