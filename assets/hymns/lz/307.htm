307. BUSIHU BO BUNDE

(SILENT NIGHT – A.H.77, C.H.102)

 

 

  1. Busihu bo bu nde, ku li tuu, kwa kanya,

    2. Fo ku pepezwi Jesu, mbututu ya kenile,

    Lobala ka kozo, lobala ka kozo

     

  2. Busihu bo bu nde, lififi la fela,

    3. Ba lisana ba utwa, Haleluya Mulena,

    Kreste U pepilwe, Kreste U pepilwe.

     

  3. Busihu bo bu nde, Jesu ki liseli,

    4. Kanya ye zwa ku Ndate, Puluso ya lifasi,

    Mulena ha pepwa, Mulena ha pepwa.

     

  4. Busihu bo bu nde, naleli ya kanya,

    5. Lubine ni mang’eloi, Haleluya ku Jesu,

    Kreste U pepilwe, Kreste U pepilwe.
