90. MUSEBEZI WA MAZAZI A MANG’WI A SILEZI

(ANOTHER SIX DAYS’ WORK – A.H.384, C.H.456, C.S.430)

 

  1. Lu felize musebezi, yak ala Sabata yeng’wi;

    2. Kuta, moyo, u to taba, ka zazi le li kenile.

     

  2. Mu te lulumbe Mulena, Ya lufa pumulo ye nde;

    3. Ku fiwa litohonolo, ka lona lizazi leo.

     

  3. Mi hupulo, buitumelo za luna li lumbe Jesu;

    4. Lu fiwe pumulo yende, ye zibiwa ki ya lumezi.

     

  4. Ku kuza koo kwa mwa pilu, ki sepiso ya pumulo,

    5. Ye beezwi ba lumeli, maungulo a manyando.
