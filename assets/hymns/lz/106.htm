106. LU KA YAHA FA LICWE

(WE’LL BUILD ON THE ROCK – A.H.213, C.H.579, C.S.32)

 

  1. Lu yahe ku Jesu mupulusi, licwe le li sa feli;

    2. Kuli moya ha u ta zuha, lu tiyele ku yena.

     

    MAKUTELO:

    Lu yahe fa licwe, lu yahe fa licwe,

    Lu ka tuyela fa licwe tota, I li yena Kreste.

    Alto,tenor,bass

    Lu ka tiyela fa licwe tota, Lu ka tiyela fa licwe tota,

    Lu ka tiyela fa licwe tota, I li yena Kreste.

     

  2. Ba bang’wi ba yaha fa lishabati, la maluwo a ‘fasi

    3. Ba bang’wi fa mandinda a sibi, ba bang’wi fa minyaka.

     

  3. A lu yahe fa licwe la niti, ki mutomo o tiile;

    4. Sepo ya puluso ya luna, Jesu ki yena niti.
