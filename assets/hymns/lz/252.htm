252. MU MU SEPE

(ONLY TRUST HIM – C.H.567, C.S.95)

 

  1. Baezalibi, mu tahe Jesu Wa swalela,

    2. Mi U ta mi fa pumulo, ha mu sepa Yena.

     

    MAKUTELO:

    Mu mu sepe, Mu mu sepe, Mu mu sepe cwale,

    Wa pulusa, Wa pulusa, Wa  mi pulusa.

     

  2. Kakuli Jesu U shwile ku fan a mbuyoti,

    3. Mu nwele cwale mwa mali a; tapisa luli.

     

  3. Ee, Jesu ki yena niti, nzila ya pumulo;

    4. Mu mu lumele kapili, mu ta hauhelwa.

     

  4. Mu kopane ni sitopa, mi mu ye kwa kanya;

    5. Mu y’o yaha kwa halimu, ko ku tezi tabo.
