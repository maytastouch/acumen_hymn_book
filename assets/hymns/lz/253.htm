253. BONA MULISANA WA ISILAELE YA BUNOLO

(SEE ISRAEL’S GENTLE SHEPHERD – C.H.411, C.S.182)

 

 

  1. Mulis’a Isilaele u yemi a biza,

    2. Mi u biza lingunyana, kuli a li lwale.

     

  2. Mu ba lisele ba tahe, mu sib a hanisi,

    3. Kakuli Mulena U tezi ku ba pulusa.

     

  3. Mulena lu ba tisize, ka buitumelo,

    4. Ka tabo luna lu ba Hao, ni bana ba luna.
