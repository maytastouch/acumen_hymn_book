16. MAHUTU- NYANA, MAMELA

(LITTLE FEET, BE CAREFUL – A.H.459, C.S.569)

 

  1. Ni tapile mazoho, mawe, a masweu,

    2. Ni kalimile Jesu, Ku mu sebeleza.

     

    MAKUTELO:

    Mahutu, mamela, Zamaya hande,

    Ni ta sebeleza Jesu a nosi.

     

  2. Lizebe za ka, utwa, lizazi kaufela,

    3. Ka likezo za musa, z’o kona ku eza.

     

  3. Meto a ka a lisa, mazoho, mahutu,

    4. Ku a sabisa bubi, ka ‘baka la Jesu.
