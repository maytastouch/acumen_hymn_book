263. MU TISE BULISHUMI KAUFELA

(BRING YE ALL THE TITHES – C.S.239)

 

 

  1. Utwa manzwi a Bibele a kale,

    2. Mutise bulishumi kaufela,

    Mu ineele kamita ni mita,

    Mu nze mu sepa mbuyoti.

     

    MAKUTELO:

    Mu tise bulishumi kaufela,

    Jesu uli, amu ni like

    Mi ni ta sulula mbuyoti,

    Mu tokwe mwa ku i amuhelela.

     

  2. Mu bata ku ziba maata a moya,

    3. Mutise bulishumi kaufela,

    Pila ni Yena ka nako ni nako,

    Ha nze A fana mbuyoti.

     

  3. Mu tibwa kin’ ku Mulen’a mina?

    4. Mutise bulishumi kaufela,

    A mu bu tise mwa sepiselize,

    U ta sulula mbuyoti.

     

  4. Mu lapele Mulena ka nako ye,

    5. Ha mu nze mu tisa bulishumi,

    Mu kolwe sepiso mi mu ta taba;

    Mwa lipilu za luwile.

     

  5. Lipina li utwahale hande-nde;

    6. Cwale lu tisize bulishumi,

    Ku be tabo mwa lipilu za luna,

    Se lu na ni limbuyoti..
