335. MU LUMBEKE NDATE

(PRAISE YE THE FATHER – A.H.47, C.H.9, C.S.377)

 

 

 

  1. Mu lumbeke Ndate ka musa wa Hae,

    2. U tokomela bana ha ba fosa,

    Lumben’ mina mang’eloi a halimu,

    Lumben’ Jehova.

     

  2. Lumben’ Mupulusi ka musa wa Hae;

    3. Ka sishemo U mamela baketwa,

    Ba ba nca, ba supali ni banana

    Mu lumbe Jesu.

     

  3. Lumben’ moya, tuso ya Isilaele;

    4. Ya na lumilwe ku to lu fuyola,

    Ku lumbiwe Ndate, Mwana ni Moya,

    Ba lumbekiwe.
