382. MU HANE BUCWALA

(AWAY THE BOWL – C.S.585)

 

 

  1. Mezi ki sino se sinde, muhane bucwala!

    2. Bucwala ki mun’a mioko, mu hane bucwala,

    A mu tuhele bucwala, siya misunga ya bona,

    Mezi ki a mande luli, mu hane bucwala!

     

  2. Litahwa la tanga-tanga, muhane bucwala!

    3. Mi li tahisa maswabi, mu hane bucwala,

    Bana ba lona ba lapa, mo ku bata mo ba inzi,

    Ba libelezi buhobe, mu hane bucwala!

     

  3. Ha luna ku bu leka, nya; muhane bucwala!

    4. Cwale lu toya bu cwala, mu hane bucwala,

    Lu kopana ku itama, lu swalani mwa lipilu,

    Ku shenja sila mwa naha, mu hane bucwala!
