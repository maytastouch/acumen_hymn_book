    MAKUTELO

    Muluti, u lize pala, U ilize hahulu;

    Mulimu U li, U lize! Ba te ba lukuluhe.

     

     

  3. I lilise fa malundu, Mwa mishitu, mabala;

    4. Ba mawate huweleza, puluso ya sibili.

     

  4. I lilise mwa nzila, Mwa maneku a mansu;

    5. Ili, lika li lukile, Ndate Usa litezi.

     

     

  5. Zibisa ba ba imezwi, Ba ba bata Mulena;

    6. Zibisa pizo ya jesu, yeli, “A mu te ku ‘Na”.
