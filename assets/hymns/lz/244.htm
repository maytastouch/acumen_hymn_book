244. TA KU JESU

(COME TO JESUS – C.S.179)

 

 

  1. Ta ku Jesu, Ta ku Jesu, Ta ku Jesu cwale,

    2. Cwale ta ku Jesu, Ta ku Jesu cwale.

     

  2. Wa pulusa, Wa pulusa, Wa pulusa cwale,

    3. Cwale Wa pulusa, Ta ku Jesu cwale.

     

  3. U ka eza, U ka eza, U ka eza cwale,

    4. Cwale U ka eza, Ta ku Jesu cwale.

     

  4. Biza Jesu, Biza Jesu, Biza Jesu cwale,

    5. Cwale Biza Jesu, Ta ku Yena cwale.

     

  5. U litezi, U litezi, U litezi cwale,

    6. Cwale U litezi, Ta ku Yena cwale.

     

  6. U mu sepe, U mu sepe, U mu sepe cwale,

    7. Cwale U mu sepe, Ta ku Yena cwale.

     

  7. Wa fuyola, Wa fuyola, Wa fuyola cwale,

    8. Cwale Wa fuyola, Ta ku Yena cwale.
